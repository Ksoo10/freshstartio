How do I know if my home needs repainting?
Signs include:
  • Fading or dull colors
  • Peeling or cracking paint
  • Water damage or stains
  • Chalking or powdery residue
  • If it’s been 5–7 years (exteriors) or 8–10 years (interiors) since last painting
What's the typical timeline for a painting project?
  • Interior rooms: 1–2 days per room (includes prep/dry time)
  • Whole-house exterior: Project-based timeline (weather-dependent)
